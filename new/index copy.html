Development & Training

Our solutions and Training are based in build and teach what is the more modern and effective using the JAVA stack, your guides are based on these principles:

Reactive

Reactive processing is a paradigm that enables developers build non-blocking, asynchronous applications that can handle back-pressure (flow control).


Cloud

Cloud computing is the on-demand availability of computer system resources, especially data storage and computing power, without direct active management by the user

Serveless

Serverless applications take advantage of modern cloud computing capabilities and abstractions to let you focus on logic rather than on infrastructure.

microservices

Microservice architectures are the ‘new normal’. Building small, self-contained, ready to run applications can bring great flexibility and added resilience to your code.

Our values

First of all, we believe that develop a software are either an art and a science, therefore we treat our software as a piece of art where code clean, test development driven and well design solution are priorities. To reach out this state of art we follow this rules:

Customer focus

More than help our customers figure out their problems we try achieve exactly what they are looking for. For that we follow this main guides:

  • Use of agiles methodologies.
  • Focus on COD (customer-oriented development).

Managment

Our development process follow the main agile methodologies and approaches, but we are always focus on management to make the fun (development) became valuable (profitable).

Most of time the statement above seems be a dream unreachable, but for became it true we focus on right stimatives and on use of worthy tools. You can trust in our methodologies.

Agenda
